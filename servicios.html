Social Marketing

WE REDESIGN YOUR COMMUNICATION

Together with your organization, we redesign the way you communicate your impact to delight donors, investors, clients, or key alliances. Social Marketing allows for temporary or permanent campaigns to highlight the transformation you have achieved and the commitments you have made through your work.

Ideal for: Medium to highly mature organizations that understand their impact.
Deliverable: Social Marketing Manual








Impact Audit

We improve your impact model

Gain an external and objective perspective on your impact model, enabling you to make innovative changes and find the path of continuous improvement to enhance your impact and/or mitigate potential crises. If your organization already knows its impact indicators and already monitors and/or reports its impact, this service is ideal for you.

Ideal for: Mature organizations that understand their impact.
Deliverable: Impact status report and improvement suggestions.
Recommended workflow: Audit once every 12 or 18 months.








Consultancy

WE COMPLEMENT YOUR PERSPECTIVE

We address topics that are important to your organization in 45 to 95-minute work sessions. We recommend consultancy if you want to review or change:

  • Impact objectives
  • Impact indicators
  • Data collection tools
  • Impact pathway
  • Operating Model
  • Financing or business model
  • Comunication strategies
  • Social Marketing Advisory



We offer packages of 5, 8, 10, and 20 sessions with a validity of 12 months.

Ideal for: Organizations of all maturity levels.
 Deliverable: One-pager per session with covered topics and suggested resources.
