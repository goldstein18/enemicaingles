Why Endemica?

Endemica was founded in 2013 with the conviction that there are no two equal projects, communities, or realities.

Since then, it has been clear to us that we were advocating for NON-STANDARDIZATION, as trying to fit into the same indicators made us lose sight of the uniqueness of each person, project, solution, context, and moment.

We decided that our work would always be customized, making each occasion of working with an organization an opportunity to offer a solution that considered the common denominators and drew from the unique factors of each project.

Endemica embodies that unique form of adaptation and exclusivity that we see in every person, project, and organization. Today, the same conviction leads us to understand that we are a consultancy that only works with tailor-made solutions, because standardizing impact means losing what makes each proposal unique.

At Endemica, we respect the individuality and unique character of those who work with us.

Center for Social Impact

Our passion lies in working with impact in the field, in communication, and in strategies.

We are excited to develop creative models so that our clients' impact reaches as far as it needs to.

That's why we are expanding our range of services, transforming from a social consultancy to a center dedicated to promoting the use and application of impact.

At ENDEMICA, we ensure that your impact captivates stakeholders.

Our Essence

  • Vision: To be a social intelligence agency that transforms social impact into new channels of communication and funding for our clients.

  • Mission: Providing innovative services for social impact.
